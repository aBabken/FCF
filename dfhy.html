DFHY Donoghue Forlines Tactical High Yield ETF
The Donoghue Forlines Tactical High Yield ETF is an Indexed ETF that aims for consistent returns for investors seeking income distribution by:
  • Participating In The High Yield Bond Market
  • Striving To Capture The Majority Of The Upside, And Avoid The Majority Of The Downside, Of The High Yield Asset Class During A Full Credit Market Cycle
Fund Investment Objective
The U.S. Free Cash Flow Quality ETF (TTAC) seeks to outperform the Russell 3000 Index over the long term, with less volatility.
Why DFNV?
Free cash flow is a far more reliable indicator of high-quality companies. TTAC uses a transparent investment process, based on our proprietary quantitative free cash flow analysis, supplemented by rigorous fundamental analysis. This offers investors:

  • The high yield bond market:

    • Offers Generally High Coupon Rates
    • May Deliver A High Level Of Current Income

  • Our defensive “tactical” indicators provide investors with:

    • Mitigation Of Downside Volatility
    • Preservation Of Capital By Primarily Shifting Towards Intermediate Term Treasury Exposure During Market Declines
    • Less Interest Rate Sensitivity Than Higher Quality Fixed Income Asset Classes
    • Less Volatility Than Dividend Paying Stocks
