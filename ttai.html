TTAI
TTAI International Free Cash Flow Quality ETF
The TTAC U.S. Free Cash Flow Quality ETF is actively managed utilizing a blend of quantitative modeling and fundamental analysis. We identify the 80-100 quality companies in the Russell 3000 Index that have the strongest combination of:
  • Revenue Recognition
  • Liability And Asset Assessment
  • Cost Treatments (Expense Vs Capitalize)

Companies with a robust blend of these three factors tend to score highly on ESG scales.

Fund Investment Objective
The U.S. Free Cash Flow Quality ETF (TTAC) seeks to outperform the Russell 3000 Index over the long term, with less volatility.
Why TTAI?
Free cash flow is a far more reliable indicator of high-quality companies. TTAC uses a transparent investment process, based on our proprietary quantitative free cash flow analysis, supplemented by rigorous fundamental analysis. This offers investors:
  • High conviction exposure to high-quality U.S. companies
  • Strong potential for excess returns over the Russell 3000 TR Index, with lower volatility
  • A diversified portfolio where ESG factors are important inputs
